About us

Learn about the purpose, mission and vision that drive this interactive space exploration platform.

🔭 Project goal

Make exoplanet exploration accessible to anyone by combining science, open data and artificial intelligence models. Our goal is for students, enthusiasts and curious minds to interact with real NASA data and understand how new worlds are discovered.

🌟 Mission

Bring astronomy and space exploration closer to everyone through an interactive platform that allows exploring, analyzing and discovering exoplanets using real data and artificial intelligence technologies.

We aim to inspire scientific curiosity and promote hands-on learning in astrophysics and data analysis.

🌌 Vision

To be an educational and scientific reference that connects the global community with the search for other worlds, promoting understanding of the universe and supporting everyone's participation in the future of space exploration.

📊 Data sources and credits

Based on public NASA catalogs (Exoplanet Archive) and discoveries from missions such as Kepler, K2 and TESS.

This project is possible thanks to the efforts of researchers, engineers and communicators who share their knowledge with the world.
